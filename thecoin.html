4 SVG artworks (21 editions) rendered entirely by their ERC-721 contract — no IPFS, no off-chain server, no external dependency. Every line, curve and color is driven by SVG elements stored on contract.

Each piece was generated from the data and theory of Bitcoin: its supply curve, proof-of-work mechanics, network security and on-chain history and given visual form on Ethereum.

Owners can call changeStyle() on the contract to alter the underlying CSS of their piece. The original visual becomes a base layer — a fixed identity that collectors can build upon, now and indefinitely.
