entangl3d

by Takens Theorem

Ethereum is a dark forest. It can reveal wanderings and whereabouts. Patterns on its ledger are the tattlings of the forest's paths and displacements. A public forest, we can all wander with a block explorer as flashlight. A beam focused through tabulations and visuals. The structure yields narrative, at least in part: The whys of these wanderings. A beam cast into the dark forest reports on a connected network: What lost history does it reveal, and toward what unseen destination does it point?

We sometimes compete to find patterns. We might seek dangers and help potential victims. Some love wandering old woods for curiosities.

entangl3d

entangl3d is a work-in-progress visuospatial explorer for the dark forest. entangl3d projects blocks visually as a slice of time, and wallets as computational activity on those blocks. You can scroll through blocks, as if moving through time. Click around. Explore.

This website demos entangl3d. The links below lead to snapshots of our dark forest. Particular points in time, little capsules. I offer a social activity, a kind of narrative-generation game. We could playfully tease this explorer as a "walking simulator" game. Wander the capsules of past patterns. What is curious about them? Are there patterns or particulars? A moment, a curious transaction, a trend, a mania a hack a community? What does your beam reveal?

How to play?

Click the capsules of time below and wander the visuospatial explorer. There are no instructions. Scroll, drag, click; poke around in the dark forest. I welcome you to fill out the response form below. I will take all responses and build an integrated narrative report of the dark forest. What we saw. It will be shared here. There will be a participation trophy.
