by Takens Theorem

Ethereum is a dark forest. It can reveal wanderings and whereabouts. Patterns on a ledger are the tattlings of a forest's paths and displacements. A public forest, we can all wander with a block explorer as flashlight. A beam focused through tabulations and visuals. The structure yields narrative, at least in part: The whys of these wanderings. A beam cast into the dark forest reports on a connected network: What lost history does it reveal, and toward what unseen destination does it point?

We gather here. We can compete to find patterns. We might seek dangers and help potential victims. Some love wandering old woods for curiosities.

entangl3d

entangl3d is a visuospatial explorer for the dark forest. I share no instructions regarding its use. Click around. Explore.

Below are links to limited segments of our dark forest. Particular points in time, little capsules. I offer a social activity, a kind of narrative-generation game1. Explore, wander the capsules of past patterns. What is curious about them? Why did I choose these periods? Are there patterns or particulars, less obvious but equally or more interesting?

How to play?

Click the capsules of time below and wander the visuospatial explorer. There are no instructions. Scroll, drag, click; poke around in the dark forest. Participants fill out the form below as much as they like. I will take all responses and compose an integrated narrative report of the dark forest. It will be shared here. There will be a participation trophy.
