Over seventy thousand lines approximate transitional ledger actions, colors represent L1 and L2s. Circuits give way to circuits, expand collective compute. Mouseover to get links for some bridge-relevant addresses. This is an experimental interface for collectors, it renders approximately for position of links, but the rendering is vector quality SVG, feel free to zoom in.

Data and rendering are approximate but they are fully based on raw transaction data gathered from Etherscan under force-directed layout algorithm in ccNetViz and rendered, styled with p5.js by Takens. Circuitry algorithm uses jointed edge paths, algorithm by Takens.


warnings: (i) SVG is very large file, (ii) SVG strips mouseover links, (iii) tested in Brave, so render may be slightly different in other browsers, (iv) full screen mode transition may be be more irregular when displaying thousands of the paths during animation.
