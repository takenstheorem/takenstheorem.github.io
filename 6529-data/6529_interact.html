Memetics is collective. Individuals and their transgressive ideas yield something more, memetics above a foundation of singular memes. The rabbit hole is not a "hole," it's a memetic vortex, emergent collective meanings. Holes don't have gravity. Thousands of memetic lines between cards and collectors give the appearance of an emerging object at the center... it has gravity. It draws us in. Mouseover to get some links for prominent addresses. This is an experimental interface for collectors, it renders approximately for position of links, but the rendering is vector quality SVG, feel free to zoom in.


Warnings:
SVG is very large file. SVG strips mouseover links. Visual tested in Brave and may appear slightly different in other browsers due to the finer detail of the thousands of connection lines.
