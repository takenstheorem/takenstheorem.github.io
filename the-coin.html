100% on-chain SVGs rendered by ERC-721 contract. Pieces are drawn from data or theory of Bitcoin, offering a visual representation of its core definition, history and possible future. Owners can interact with the contract via changeStyle() and alter the underlying CSS of their pieces, giving the original visual a "base layer" identity to be built upon.

Each piece exists entirely on-chain — the SVG data, the rendering logic, the interactivity. A study in what it means to be truly decentralized art.

The contract allows collectors to modify the CSS of their pieces, creating a collaborative layer atop the original work. This interactive element transforms each piece from static art into a living system — a base layer waiting for personal expression.

84 pieces total, each a unique interpretation of Bitcoin's essence through the lens of on-chain data and cryptographic theory.
